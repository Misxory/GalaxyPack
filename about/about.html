Client History

Galaxy Client is a third-party resource package client for Minecraft PE 0.14.3 developed and maintained by _outice and his team, which has been continuously updated and supported since its birth in 2022 ( formerly "Icecraft" ), and has now developed into an excellent client among many Minecraft PE 0.14.3 third-party clients.

The client includes a wide range of features and a beautiful and simple user interface to give you the best possible experience in this client.

Team Infos

The team of Galaxy Client is composed of _outice ( master & developer ), hsh (developer ), creeperboy604 ( developer ), minqwq ( helper ) and others, and our philosophy is: to contribute to the development of Minecraft PE 0.14.3.
